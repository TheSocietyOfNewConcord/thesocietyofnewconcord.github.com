  • Artworks Artworks For and About New Concord 

    • Drawings, Paintings, Photographs

    For a small place, New Concord is home to a number of creative people—some who work in the arts professionally, and some who are involved as hobbyists. We have professional photographers and people who take weekend snapshots; painters who sell and show, and people who dash off sketch drawings. We are also lucky enough to have fallen heir to some historical photographs of the hamlet that give us a hint of how things looked many years ago.

    Members and Friends of the New Concord community are encouraged to submit art works for display on this website for the enjoyment of our neighbors. Send them electronically to
